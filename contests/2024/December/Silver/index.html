MooSA Computing Olympiad
MOOSACO 2024 December Contest, Problem 1, Party Time Bessie is trying to host a party! However, because Bessie is peculiar she wants there to either be \(r\space(1\leq r \leq 10)\) people who know each other or \(s\space(1 \leq s \leq 10)\) people who don't. As Bessie is also an introvert, help Bessie calculate the smallest number \(R(r, s)\) such that her peculiarities are satisified. Input Format The first line will contain two integers, \(r\) and \(s\) Output Format Print out N lines. For line I, print out YES if cow I is racist and NO if cow I is not racist. Sample Input 3 3 Sample Output 6 This is the solution to the famous problem: "How many people do you need to invite to a party such that there are either 3 people who all know each other or 3 people that don't." Scoring Inputs 1-10: No further restrictions. P1 P2 P3 P4 P5 P6 P7 P8 P9 P10
