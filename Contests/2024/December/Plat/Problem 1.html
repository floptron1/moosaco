MooSA Computing Olympiad
MOOSACO 2024 December Contest, Pointy Stick Problem 1, Bessie Traveling Farmer John wants to sell milk to \(N\space(1 \leq N \leq20)\) barns, including his own. He puts Bessie in charge of selling the \(N\) bottles of milk to the \(N\) barns starting at Farmer John's farm, Barn 1. However, Farmer Nhoj stole Bessie's travel plans the night before she left! As the milk spoils easily, help Bessie find the shortest path which visits all of the barns and returns to Farmer John's barn. You are given \(\frac{N\cdot(N-1)}{2}\) distances in the form \(a_i, b_i, c_i\space(1 \leq a_i < b_i \leq N,\space 1 \leq c_i \leq 10^9)\), where \(c_i\) is the distance between barns \(a_i\) and \(b_i\). Input Format The first line of the integer will contain N. The following \(\frac{N\cdot(N-1)}{2}\) lines will contain \(a_i, b_i, c_i\). Output Format Print out the shortest possible path. Sample Input 2 1 2 3 Sample Output 6 Bessie starts at Barn 1. She has to travel to Barn 2 and back to Barn 1, traveling 3 each time for 6 traveled in total. Scoring Inputs 1-5: \(N \leq 10\) Inputs 6-10: No further restrictions. P1 P2 P3 P4 P5 P6 P7 P8 P9 P10
